4.0 Case Selection and Research Methods 4.0 案例选择与研究方法 4.0 Case Selection and Research Methods
案例选择与研究方法

Before we step into Piraeus and onto the Budapest–Belgrade line, a word on how and why they were selected. This section employs a case selection strategy combining Most Similar Systems Design (MSSD) with Most Different Outcomes. 在我们步入比雷埃夫斯和布达佩斯-贝尔格莱德线之前，先说明它们是如何以及为何被选择的。本节采用结合最相似系统设计(MSSD)与最不同结果的案例选择策略。

4.0.1 The Logic of "Most Similar" and "Most Different" Case Selection

Core message: Under highly similar backgrounds, the two projects exhibit diametrically opposite outcomes—providing a strong starting point for causal inference. 核心信息：在高度相似的背景下，两个项目呈现出截然相反的结果——为因果推断提供了强有力的起点。

Extreme Case Value

The two cases represent "most-likely" and "least-likely" scenarios. Piraeus, as a privatization project with clear commercial logic, had high success expectations; the Budapest-Belgrade Railway, as a cross-border public project involving multi-state coordination and EU review, faced greater difficulty. The contrast tests the explanatory boundaries of the theoretical framework. 这两个案例代表了"最可能"和"最不可能"的情景。比雷埃夫斯作为具有明确商业逻辑的私有化项目，成功预期较高；布达佩斯-贝尔格莱德铁路作为涉及多国协调和欧盟审查的跨境公共项目，面临更大困难。这种对比检验了理论框架的解释边界。

4.0.2 Analytical Technique: Theory-Guided Process Tracing

This chapter employs theory-guided process tracing. The core characteristics of this method include: (a) prior theoretical expectations based on the "three ideal types" from Chapter 3; (b) identification of critical decision nodes in project timelines; (c) evidence testing through process evidence. 本章采用理论指导的过程追踪法。该方法的核心特征包括：(a)基于第三章"三种理想类型"的先验理论预期；(b)识别项目时间线中的关键决策节点；(c)通过过程证据进行证据检验。

Process Tracing Evidence Standards

Smoking-gun Highly specific evidence strongly supporting particular theories
Hoop Basic tests that theory must pass; requires cross-validation
Straw-in-wind Supports expectation but could be explained by other theories
Doubly-decisive Supports one theory while excluding alternatives (rare in social science)

Application in This Study

  • • Evidence labels appear in case analysis tables
  • • Purpose: inviting readers to judge whether evidence supports inferences
  • • Multi-source triangulation for reliability
  • • Explicit acknowledgment of information gaps

Note on Evidence Presentation: The purpose of labeling is not to declare "I have found the smoking gun," but to open the analysis to reader scrutiny—inviting readers to judge whether the evidence presented is sufficient to support the inferences drawn. 关于证据呈现的说明：标记的目的不在于宣称"我找到了确凿证据"，而是向读者的审视开放分析——邀请读者判断所呈现的证据是否足以支持所做出的推断。

4.0.3 Data Sources and Limitations

Primary Data Sources

  • Official documents (project cooperation agreements, EU Commission investigation reports)
  • Corporate annual reports (COSCO Shipping, Piraeus Port Authority)
  • Media coverage (Financial Times, Reuters, Xinhua, local media)

4.1 Introduction to the EU's Connectivity Strategy 4.1 欧盟联通战略介绍 4.1 Introduction to the EU's Connectivity Strategy
欧盟联通战略介绍

The European Union's Global Gateway strategy was formally launched in December 2021, constituting the EU's strategic response to global infrastructure investment demands and, explicitly, to the Belt and Road Initiative. 欧盟的全球门户(Global Gateway)战略于2021年12月正式启动，构成了欧盟对全球基础设施投资需求的战略回应，并且明确针对"一带一路"倡议。

The strategy targets the mobilization of €300 billion in investment by 2027, integrating resources from EU institutions, member states, development finance institutions, the European Investment Bank (EIB), and the European Bank for Reconstruction and Development (EBRD) through the "Team Europe" mechanism. 该战略目标是到2027年动员3000亿欧元投资，通过"欧洲团队"(Team Europe)机制整合欧盟机构、成员国、开发性金融机构、欧洲投资银行(EIB)和欧洲复兴开发银行(EBRD)的资源。

A defining feature of the Global Gateway is its values-driven investment philosophy. Unlike the BRI's emphasis on "no political strings attached," the Global Gateway binds investment to principles of democratic values, good governance, transparency, and environmental sustainability. Its six guiding principles comprise: 全球门户的一个决定性特征是价值驱动的投资理念。与BRI强调"不附加政治条件"不同，全球门户将投资与民主价值观、良治、透明度和环境可持续性的原则绑定。其六项指导原则包括：

Six Guiding Principles of Global Gateway

1. Democratic values and high standards
2. Good governance and transparency
3. Equal partnership
4. Green and clean development
5. Security orientation
6. Catalyzing private sector investment

Implementation Reality: Five years in, the gap between promise and delivery is hard to ignore. Large-scale cross-border projects remain scarce; disbursements are cautious; attention has drifted toward energy transition at the expense of physical connectivity. The moniker sticks: "graveyard of hundreds of billions of euros." 实施现实：五年过去，承诺与交付之间的差距难以忽视。大规模跨境项目仍然稀缺；支出谨慎；注意力已从实体联通转向能源转型。这个绰号流传开来："数千亿欧元的墓地"。

Source: European Commission (2021), "Global Gateway: Connecting Europe and Asia." 来源：欧盟委员会(2021)，《全球门户：连接欧洲与亚洲》。

4.2 Objectives of the EU's Connectivity Strategy 4.2 欧盟联通战略的目标 4.2 Objectives of the EU's Connectivity Strategy
欧盟联通战略的目标

The Global Gateway strategy encompasses five investment domains, each with explicit policy objectives. Both initiatives talk about transport, energy, and digital infrastructure—the same words, but different meanings. 全球门户战略涵盖五个投资领域，每个领域都有明确的政策目标。两个倡议都谈论交通、能源和数字基础设施——同样的词汇，但不同的含义。

4.2.1 Digital Connectivity

The digital domain targets support for developing countries in deploying secure, resilient digital infrastructure. The MEDUSA project, a flagship digital initiative, aims to reduce latency and enhance redundancy through open-access connections between North Africa and Europe. 数字领域旨在支持发展中国家部署安全、有弹性的数字基础设施。MEDUSA项目作为旗舰数字倡议，旨在通过北非与欧洲之间的开放接入连接减少延迟并增强冗余。

Submarine cables Local data centers Cybersecurity capacity Digital skills training

4.2.2 Energy Connectivity

The energy domain centers on driving the green transition. The EU-Namibia partnership serves as a paradigm case—integrating port and logistics upgrades, electrolyzer manufacturing capacity, industrial standards, and local training to achieve a "double dividend": securing European critical raw material supplies while advancing African local value chain development. 能源领域以推动绿色转型为中心。欧盟-纳米比亚伙伴关系是一个典范案例——整合港口和物流升级、电解槽制造能力、工业标准和当地培训，以实现"双重红利"：确保欧洲关键原材料供应，同时推进非洲本地价值链发展。

Priority areas: Solar, wind, green hydrogen projects | Alignment with Paris Agreement objectives

4.2.3 Transport Connectivity

The transport domain focuses on constructing cross-border transport corridors. The Lobito Corridor represents the Global Gateway's flagship transport project in Africa, connecting the copper-cobalt mining belt of the Democratic Republic of Congo and Zambia to the Atlantic port of Lobito in Angola. 交通领域专注于建设跨境交通走廊。洛比托走廊是全球门户在非洲的旗舰交通项目，连接刚果民主共和国和赞比亚的铜钴矿带至安哥拉大西洋港口洛比托。

Lobito Corridor: Reducing transport time, minimizing losses, catalyzing processing industry
TEN-T Alignment: Supporting transport infrastructure upgrades in Western Balkans and Eastern partners

4.2.4 Health Connectivity

The Global Gateway incorporates health infrastructure into the connectivity framework, supporting local vaccine production, pharmaceutical regulatory system development, and medical supply chain resilience enhancement. This direction has gained prominence following the COVID-19 pandemic, reflecting the EU's strategic concerns regarding global health security. 全球门户将卫生基础设施纳入联通框架，支持本地疫苗生产、药品监管系统发展和医疗供应链韧性增强。这一方向在COVID-19大流行后获得突出地位，反映了欧盟对全球卫生安全的战略关切。

4.2.5 Education and Research Connectivity

Through programs such as Erasmus+, the Global Gateway supports youth exchange, higher education cooperation, and research network development, aiming to cultivate local talent and promote knowledge sharing. 通过伊拉斯谟+(Erasmus+)等项目，全球门户支持青年交流、高等教育合作和研究网络发展，旨在培养本地人才并促进知识共享。

"From these objectives, a pattern emerges. The BRI speaks the language of hardware: roads, ports, railways, power stations. The Global Gateway speaks the language of software: standards, governance, transparency, local capacity building. One builds the machine; the other writes the manual. When these two approaches meet on the same project—as they do in the cases that follow—the result is not just divergence but drama." "从这些目标中，一种模式浮现出来。BRI说着硬件的语言：道路、港口、铁路、电站。全球门户说着软件的语言：标准、治理、透明度、本地能力建设。一个建造机器；另一个编写手册。当这两种方法在同一个项目上相遇时——正如我们在后续案例中将看到的——结果不仅是分歧，而是戏剧性的冲突。"
