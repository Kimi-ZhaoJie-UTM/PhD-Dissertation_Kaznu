Executive Summary 执行摘要
"Imagine you are a finance minister in Astana or Athens. On your desk lie two proposals. One, from Beijing, promises rapid construction... The other, from Brussels, comes with a thick binder of environmental safeguards..."
"想象一下，您是阿斯塔纳或雅典的财政部长。桌上放着两份提案。一份来自北京，承诺快速建设……另一份来自布鲁塞尔，附带厚厚一叠环境保障措施文件……"

This dissertation conceptualizes the BRI–Global Gateway nexus as a case of regime coopetition—a space where competition and complementarity coexist. Drawing on institutional complexity theory, I construct a novel typology of regulatory interaction, distinguishing between three ideal-typical outcomes: adaptive coordination, structural confrontation, and institutional arbitrage.

本论文将一带一路与全球门户的关系概念化为制度竞合的案例——一个竞争与互补共存的空间。基于制度复杂性理论，我构建了一个新的监管交互类型学，区分了三种理想类型结果：适应性协调、结构性对抗和制度套利。

Through theory-guided process tracing of two polar cases—the Port of Piraeus (harmonious coordination) and the Budapest–Belgrade railway (conflictual confrontation)—this study reveals how rule divergences translate into financial reality on the ground.

通过对两个极性案例的理论导向过程追踪——比雷埃夫斯港（和谐协调）和布达佩斯-贝尔格莱德铁路（冲突对抗）——本研究揭示了规则差异如何转化为现实的金融结果。

Five Core Findings 五大核心发现

Moving beyond the "China-versus-EU" geopolitics to reveal the micro-mechanisms of institutional operation 超越"中国对抗欧盟"的地缘政治叙事，揭示制度运作的微观机制

01

Institutional Arbitrage Space 制度套利空间

Institutional overlap creates genuine arbitrage opportunities, enabling host states to optimize financing by cherry-picking advantageous rules from both BRI and Global Gateway.

制度重叠创造了真正的套利机会，使东道国能够通过从一带一路和全球门户中挑选有利规则来优化融资。

See Chapter 3.2 见第3.2章
02

Compliance Cost Stacking 合规成本叠加

When projects rely on multi-source funding, compliance costs exhibit non-linear multiplication effects—spiraling beyond control when rule-setting parties exceed three.

当项目依赖多源融资时，合规成本呈现非线性乘数效应——当规则制定方超过三方时，成本会螺旋式失控。

See Chapter 3.3 见第3.3章
03

Morphology as Choice 形态即选择

Rule interaction is not fate but choice: Piraeus succeeded through proactive adaptive compliance, while Budapest–Belgrade failed due to cumulative failures in technical standard alignment.

规则交互不是命运而是选择：比雷埃夫斯通过主动适应性合规取得成功，而布达佩斯-贝尔格莱德因技术标准协调的累积失败而受挫。

See Chapter 4 见第4章
04

Regulatory Bricolage 监管拼凑

Through pragmatic recombination of rules—what I call regulatory bricolage—Pareto improvements reconciling efficiency and compliance are achievable.

通过规则的务实重组——我称之为监管拼凑——可以实现兼顾效率与合规的帕累托改进。

See Chapter 5.2.3 见第5.2.3章
05

The Middle Ground Matters 中间地带的重要性

"Middle-ground" actors in Central Asia and beyond—neither fully aligned with Beijing nor with Brussels—shape the future of Eurasian connectivity through strategies of regulatory bricolage. Kazakhstan's hedging strategy exemplifies this agency.

中亚及其他地区的"中间地带"行为者——既不完全倾向于北京也不完全倾向于布鲁塞尔——通过监管拼凑策略塑造欧亚互联互通的未来。哈萨克斯坦的对冲策略是这种能动性的典型例证。
