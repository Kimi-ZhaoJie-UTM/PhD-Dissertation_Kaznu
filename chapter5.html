5.1 Summary of Key Findings / 主要发现总结

Before diving into the detailed summary, let me state the core contributions in plain language—the kind you might use to explain your thesis to a colleague over coffee.

First, institutional overlap is not just a problem; it's also an opportunity. The coexistence of BRI and Global Gateway rules creates space for what I call institutional arbitrage—participating countries can pick and choose between rule systems to optimize their financing deals.

Second, when rules collide, costs don't just add up—they multiply. The Budapest–Belgrade railway is a cautionary tale: with five different financiers each imposing their own rules, compliance costs spiraled exponentially, not linearly.

在深入详细总结之前，让我用通俗的语言陈述核心贡献——那种你可以在喝咖啡时向同事解释论文的语言。

第一，制度重叠不仅仅是问题；它也是机遇。BRI与全球门户规则的共存创造了制度套利的空间——参与国可以在规则体系之间挑选选择，以优化其融资交易。

第二，当规则碰撞时，成本不仅仅是相加——而是相乘。布达佩斯-贝尔格莱德铁路是一个警示故事：五个不同的融资方各自施加自己的规则，合规成本呈指数级螺旋上升，而非线性增长。

Third, "speed" and "standards" are not enemies. The Piraeus port shows that Chinese investment can move fast while still meeting EU standards—if the investor is willing to adapt proactively. This is adaptive compliance: not just ticking boxes, but embedding oneself in the local institutional fabric.

Fourth, the real action is in the "middle ground." Countries like Kazakhstan, Serbia, and Indonesia—neither fully aligned with Beijing nor with Brussels—are the ones that matter most. Their strategies of regulatory bricolage—patching together rules from different sources—will shape the future of Eurasian connectivity.

第三，"速度"与"标准"并非敌人。比雷埃夫斯港表明，中国投资可以快速推进，同时仍然满足欧盟标准——前提是投资者愿意主动适应。这就是适应性合规：不仅仅是打勾画圈，而是将自己嵌入当地制度结构中。

第四，真正的行动在"中间地带"。像哈萨克斯坦、塞尔维亚和印度尼西亚这样的国家——既不完全与北京对齐，也不完全与布鲁塞尔对齐——才是最重要的。它们的监管拼凑策略——将来自不同来源的规则拼凑在一起——将塑造欧亚互联互通的未来。

5.1.1 BRI and Global Gateway Form an Institutional Coopetition Relationship / BRI与全球门户形成制度竞合关系

The BRI and Global Gateway share similarities in objectives—both are committed to promoting transnational infrastructure connectivity—yet exhibit fundamental differences in rule design, implementation logic, and value orientation. This relationship can be characterized as "institutional coopetition": competition across dimensions including financing mechanisms, environmental standards, procurement procedures, debt sustainability, transparency, and implementation efficiency; yet potential synergies exist in domains such as green finance, climate change, and third-country market cooperation.

This coopetition relationship constitutes the most significant institutional phenomenon in current international development finance, exerting profound influence on the strategic choices of participating countries.

BRI与全球门户在目标上具有相似性——都致力于促进跨国基础设施互联互通——但在规则设计、实施逻辑和价值取向上表现出根本差异。这种关系可以定性为"制度竞合"：在融资机制、环境标准、采购程序、债务可持续性、透明度和实施效率等维度上存在竞争；但在绿色金融、气候变化和第三方市场合作等领域存在潜在协同效应。

这种竞合关系构成了当前国际发展融资中最重大的制度现象，对参与国的战略选择产生深远影响。

5.1.3 Mechanisms Through Which Rule Differences Shape Opportunities and Risks / 规则差异塑造机遇与风险的机制

Rule differences generate differentiated financial opportunities and risks for different actors:

For Participating Countries / 对参与国

The coexistence of two rule systems provides "institutional arbitrage" space—enabling selection or combination of advantageous rules according to their own needs, enhancing bargaining power, and obtaining diversified financing.

For Chinese Enterprises / 对中国企业

The BRI's "speed advantage" and industrial chain synergies create opportunities for rapid market entry, while renminbi internationalization instruments reduce exchange rate risks.

For European Enterprises / 对欧洲企业

The Global Gateway's "standard advantages" provide differentiated competitiveness, creating opportunities in green finance and compliance consulting.

规则差异为不同行为者创造了差异化的金融机遇与风险：

对参与国

两种规则体系的共存提供了"制度套利"空间——使其能够根据自身需求选择或组合有利规则，增强议价能力，获得多元化融资。

对中国企业

BRI的"速度优势"和产业链协同效应创造了快速进入市场的机遇，而人民币国际化工具则降低了汇率风险。

对欧洲企业

全球门户的"标准优势"提供了差异化竞争力，在绿色金融和合规咨询领域创造机遇。

5.1.5 Case Validation: Possibilities and Challenges of Rule Coordination / 案例验证：规则协调的可能性与挑战

Piraeus Case: Success Through Adaptation

The Piraeus case demonstrates that when Chinese investors proactively adapt to EU rules and respect local governance, BRI projects can succeed in Europe. Through compliance with EU environmental standards, competition law, and transparency requirements, the project achieved compatibility between "speed advantage" and "standard advantage."

比雷埃夫斯案例：通过适应获得成功

比雷埃夫斯案例表明，当中国投资者主动适应欧盟规则并尊重当地治理时，BRI项目可以在欧洲取得成功。通过遵守欧盟环境标准、竞争法和透明度要求，该项目实现了"速度优势"与"标准优势"的兼容性。

Budapest-Belgrade: Cautionary Tale of Conflict

The Budapest-Belgrade Railway case highlights the negative effects of rule conflicts. Multiplicity of financing sources, complexity of standard applicability, and uncertainty of EU review have jointly produced severe delays. This case warns that when institutional coordination is insufficient, infrastructure projects may become sacrifices to rule competition.

布达佩斯-贝尔格莱德：冲突的警示故事

布达佩斯-贝尔格莱德铁路案例凸显了规则冲突的负面效应。融资来源的多样性、标准适用性的复杂性以及欧盟审查的不确定性共同导致了严重延误。此案例警告，当制度协调不足时，基础设施项目可能成为规则竞争的牺牲品。

5.1.6 Dynamic Evolution of the EU Response / 欧盟回应的动态演进

The EU's response to the BRI has evolved from "observation and engagement" to "strategic competition." The launch of the Global Gateway, intensification of FDI screening, implementation of Net-Zero Industry Act non-price criteria, and upgrading of the economic security strategy collectively constitute the EU's "rule countermeasure" policy toolkit.

欧盟对BRI的回应已从"观察与接触"演进为"战略竞争"。全球门户的启动、外国直接投资审查的强化、净零产业法非价格标准的实施以及经济安全战略的升级，共同构成了欧盟的"规则对策"政策工具包。

5.2 Implications and Policy Recommendations / 启示与政策建议

From Academic Analysis to Policy Action

Analysis without action is incomplete. The question now is: what do these findings mean for the people who actually build infrastructure, sign loans, and navigate rules? Three sets of actors, three sets of recommendations.

从学术分析到政策行动

没有行动的分析是不完整的。现在的问题是：这些发现对那些实际建设基础设施、签署贷款和驾驭规则的人意味着什么？三组行为者，三组建议。

5.2.1 Recommendations for Chinese Enterprises and Policymakers / 对中国企业和决策者的建议

Recommendation I: Compliance Capacity Building Roadmap

Short-term (2025-2027): Establish EU rule tracking mechanisms; designate dedicated compliance officers; subscribe to EU regulatory updates.

Medium-term (2027-2030): Internalize EU standards into corporate processes; establish cooperation with EU certification bodies; train local compliance teams.

Long-term (2030+): Participate in EU standards consultation; promote standard mutual recognition clauses in bilateral agreements.

Recommendation II: Explore "Speed-Standards" Complementary Models

Applicable in third-country markets (Africa, Central Asia). Chinese role: Rapid construction, equipment supply. European role: Green certification, environmental due diligence. Benefit distribution: 60-70% Chinese, 30-40% European based on risk-contribution.

建议一：合规能力建设路线图

短期(2025-2027)：建立欧盟规则跟踪机制；指定专门合规官；订阅欧盟监管更新。

中期(2027-2030)：将欧盟标准内化到企业流程；与欧盟认证机构建立合作；培训本地合规团队。

长期(2030+)：参与欧盟标准咨询；在双边协议中推动标准互认条款。

建议二：探索"速度-标准"互补模式

适用于第三方市场（非洲、中亚）。中国角色：快速建设、设备供应。欧洲角色：绿色认证、环境尽职调查。利益分配：根据风险贡献，中国60-70%，欧洲30-40%。

5.2.2 Recommendations for EU Policymakers / 对欧盟决策者的建议

Recommendation I: Implement "Global Gateway 2.0" Process Reengineering

Core problem: Current Global Gateway "high standards but slow deployment"—implementation lag undermines strategic effectiveness.

  • Due Diligence: Establish 18-month time limits; conduct reviews in parallel rather than sequential
  • Private Capital Mobilization: Expand ACLB guarantee coverage to Asia; introduce "construction-period insurance"
  • Standard Application: Establish "graduated compliance" mechanisms allowing phased achievement
  • Coordination with BRI: Establish "China-EU Infrastructure Coordination Offices" in Africa and Central Asia

建议一：实施"全球门户2.0"流程再造

核心问题：当前全球门户"高标准但部署缓慢"——实施滞后削弱战略效果。

  • 尽职调查：设立18个月时限；并行而非顺序进行审查
  • 私人资本动员：将ACLB担保覆盖范围扩展至亚洲；引入"建设期保险"
  • 标准应用：建立"分级合规"机制，允许分阶段达标
  • 与BRI协调：在非洲和中亚设立"中欧基础设施协调办公室"

5.2.3 Recommendations for Participating Countries: From Arbitrage to Bricolage / 对参与国的建议：从套利到拼凑

Throughout this dissertation, I have used the term "institutional arbitrage" to describe how participating countries exploit rule differentials. But I now propose a complementary concept: regulatory bricolage—the pragmatic recombination of rules from different sources to craft a workable project structure.

Strategic Recommendations:

  1. Optimize "Institutional Arbitrage" Strategies: Move from passive selection to active project-level rule combination design
  2. Establish Project-Level Risk Monitoring Frameworks: Track debt risk, exchange rate risk, geopolitical risk, and environmental/social risk
  3. Leverage Regional Influence: Promote China-EU rule coordination through regional dialogue platforms

在本论文中，我一直使用"制度套利"一词来描述参与国如何利用规则差异。但我现在提出一个补充概念：监管拼凑——将来自不同来源的规则进行务实重组，以构建可行的项目结构。

战略建议：

  1. 优化"制度套利"策略：从被动选择转向主动的项目级规则组合设计
  2. 建立项目级风险监测框架：跟踪债务风险、汇率风险、地缘政治风险及环境/社会风险
  3. 利用区域影响力：通过区域对话平台促进中欧规则协调

5.3 Limitations and Future Research / 研究局限性与未来研究方向

Limitation I: Limited Case Quantity

Only two European cases, both transportation infrastructure. Transformation: Future research could expand to energy and digital infrastructure.

Limitation II: Dependence on Public Sources

No field interviews or internal documents. Transformation: Employ semi-structured elite interviews (suggested sample: 15-20 persons per case).

Limitation III: Temporal Constraints

Global Gateway short implementation period. Transformation: Establish panel study design, updating case progress every 2-3 years.

局限性一：案例数量有限

仅两个欧洲案例，均为交通基础设施。转化：未来研究可扩展至能源和数字基础设施。

局限性二：依赖公开资料

无实地访谈或内部文件。转化：采用半结构化精英访谈（建议样本：每案例15-20人）。

局限性三：时间限制

全球门户实施期较短。转化：建立面板研究设计，每2-3年更新案例进展。

Future Research Directions

  • Expand case scope to Africa and Asia for cross-regional comparison
  • Conduct field investigations and in-depth interviews
  • Quantitative analysis using HLM or fsQCA methods
  • Track Global Gateway 2.0 evolution
  • Deepen country heterogeneity research

未来研究方向

  • 扩展案例范围至非洲和亚洲进行跨区域比较
  • 进行实地调查和深度访谈
  • 使用HLM或fsQCA方法进行定量分析
  • 跟踪全球门户2.0演进
  • 深化国家异质性研究

5.4 Concluding Remarks: Theoretical Contributions and Practical Significance / 结语：理论贡献与实践意义

5.4.1 Contributions to Institutional Complexity Theory / 对制度复杂性理论的贡献

  1. Constructing a "typology of rule interaction": Moving beyond descriptive classification, proposing three ideal types—coordination, confrontation, and arbitrage—with formative causal conditions.
  2. Introducing "institutional density" as moderating variable: The intensity of institutional constraints significantly affects the manifestation of rule interaction types.
  3. Revealing "learning effects" of institutional competition: Evidence of bidirectional institutional learning between BRI and Global Gateway, yet core differences exhibit path dependence.
  1. 构建"规则互动类型学"：超越描述性分类，提出三种理想类型——协调、对抗和套利——及其形成的因果条件。
  2. 引入"制度密度"作为调节变量：制度约束的强度显著影响规则互动类型的表现。
  3. 揭示制度竞争的"学习效应"：BRI与全球门户之间存在双向制度学习的证据，但核心差异表现出路径依赖。

5.4.2 Implications for Development Finance / 对发展融资的启示

  • Challenging the "debt trap" narrative: Revealing heterogeneity of BRI debt effects—long-term potential to reduce debt-to-GDP ratios, with short-term liquidity as key moderating variable.
  • Proposing "multi-source financing coordination dilemma": When projects involve Chinese financing, EU funds, and MDB loans, rule mutual exclusivity may generate a "compliance impossible triangle."
  • Emphasizing the market value of "compliance premium": Proactive adaptation to high-standard rules can achieve long-term commercial success.
  • 挑战"债务陷阱"叙事：揭示BRI债务效应的异质性——长期可能降低债务/GDP比率，短期流动性是关键调节变量。
  • 提出"多源融资协调困境"：当项目涉及中国融资、欧盟资金和多边开发银行贷款时，规则互斥可能产生"合规不可能三角"。
  • 强调"合规溢价"的市场价值：主动适应高标准规则可以实现长期商业成功。

5.4.3 Contributions to China-EU Relations Research / 对中欧关系研究的贡献

  • Transcending the "geopolitical confrontation" narrative: Characterizing BRI-Global Gateway as "institutional coopetition" rather than zero-sum confrontation.
  • Revealing the critical role of "rule coordination": Rule coordination is a necessary condition for BRI success in Europe, not merely relying on economic scale.
  • Focusing on agency of "middle ground countries": Small and medium-sized countries are not passive recipients but actors with strategic autonomy.
  • 超越"地缘政治对抗"叙事：将BRI-全球门户定性为"制度竞合"而非零和对抗。
  • 揭示"规则协调"的关键作用：规则协调是BRI在欧洲成功的必要条件，而非仅仅依赖经济规模。
  • 关注"中间地带国家"的主体性：中小国家不是被动接受者，而是具有战略自主性的行为者。

5.4.4 Practical Significance and Final Appeal / 实践意义与最后呼吁

"Let me end where I began: with the finance minister in Astana, staring at two proposals on her desk. One promises speed, the other standards. One comes with no strings attached, the other with a thick binder of conditions. Which should she choose?"

This dissertation has offered no simple answer—because there is none. But it has offered a way of thinking: not as a binary between China and Europe, but as strategic navigation of institutional complexity. Not as a zero-sum game, but as an opportunity for regulatory bricolage.

What matters is not who builds the road, but that the road gets built.

"让我回到起点：阿斯塔纳的财政部长，盯着桌上的两份提案。一份承诺速度，另一份承诺标准。一份不附加条件，另一份附带厚厚的条件 binder。她应该选择哪一个？"

本论文没有提供简单的答案——因为本就没有。但它提供了一种思维方式：不是作为中国与欧洲之间的二元对立，而是作为制度复杂性的战略导航。不是作为零和游戏，而是作为监管拼凑的机遇。

重要的不是谁修建了道路，而是道路是否建成。

The competition between BRI and Global Gateway has consumed diplomatic energy that could have been spent on building. My hope is that this research—modest as it is—might help shift the conversation from rivalry to coordination, from institutional competition to regulatory complementarity. Because in the end, the benefits of connectivity must reach those who need them most.
