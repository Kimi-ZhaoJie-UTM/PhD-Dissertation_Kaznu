A Textbook Example of Harmonious Coordination 适应性协调的教科书式范例 A Textbook Example of Harmonious Coordination / 适应性协调的教科书式范例

Twenty kilometers southwest of Athens, where the Saronic Gulf meets the Aegean, lies a port that has become an unlikely symbol of China-Europe cooperation. Piraeus is not just Greece's largest port; it is the place where the BRI's ambitions met Europe's regulatory realities—and, against the odds, found a way to coexist. 雅典西南二十公里处，萨罗尼克湾与爱琴海交汇处，坐落着一个港口，它已成为中欧合作的一个出人意料的象征。比雷埃夫斯不仅仅是希腊最大的港口；它是BRI的雄心与欧洲监管现实相遇的地方——而且，尽管困难重重，找到了共存之道。

When COSCO first arrived in 2008, few predicted success. The port was underperforming, its infrastructure aging, its workforce skeptical of foreign ownership. The Greek economy was about to implode. Yet by 2025, Piraeus had transformed: from 880,000 TEU to 6.5 million, from a Mediterranean backwater to Europe's fourth-largest container port. 当中远集团于2008年首次抵达时，很少有人预料到会成功。港口表现不佳，基础设施老化，其劳动力对外国所有权持怀疑态度。希腊经济即将崩溃。然而到2025年，比雷埃夫斯已经转型：从88万TEU到650万TEU，从地中海的落后地区到欧洲第四大集装箱港口。

4.3.1 Project Background and Historical Evolution

The Piraeus project traversed a decade-long tortuous trajectory, marked by crisis, political contestation, and ultimate revival. Understanding this timeline is crucial for appreciating how rule adaptation occurred not as a single decision but as a cumulative process.

比雷埃夫斯项目走过了长达十年的曲折轨迹，经历了危机、政治争议和最终复兴。理解这一时间线对于领会规则适应如何发生至关重要——它不是单一决策，而是一个累积过程。

Key Timeline / 关键时间线

Phase I: Concession Acquisition (2008–2010)

COSCO Group signed 35-year concession for Piers II and III. On October 1, 2010, formally assumed control. 中远集团签署II号和III号码头35年特许经营权。2010年10月1日正式接管。

Phase II: Crisis and Contestation (2010–2015)

Greek sovereign debt crisis (2010). In early 2015, left-wing Syriza government suspended privatization, reviewing foreign investment. Critical juncture: Project momentarily stalled. 希腊主权债务危机(2010)。2015年初，左翼激进联盟政府暂停私有化，审查外国投资。关键节点：项目一度停滞。

Phase III: Majority Equity Acquisition (2016)

April 8: COSCO Shipping signed agreement for 67% stake (€368.5m). June 30: Greek Parliament approved 223-25. August 10: Transaction completed. 4月8日：中远海运签署67%股权协议(3.685亿欧元)。6月30日：希腊议会以223-25批准。8月10日：交易完成。

Phase IV: Rapid Development (2016–Present)

October 2021: Raised stake to 67% (full acquisition). By 2024: 6.5 million TEU throughput, Mediterranean's largest port. 2021年10月：持股比例提升至67%(完全收购)。到2024年：吞吐量650万TEU，地中海最大港口。

Critical Observation / 关键观察

The 2015 suspension by the Syriza government represents a crucial counterfactual moment. If Greek government support were unconditional, such suspension would not have occurred. The project was restarted precisely because COSCO demonstrated rule-adaptation willingness during negotiations, not because of unilateral government concessions. 2015年激进联盟政府的暂停代表了关键的反事实时刻。如果希腊政府的支持是无条件的，这种暂停就不会发生。项目之所以能够重启，正是因为中远在谈判期间展示了规则适应意愿，而非由于单方面的政府让步。

4.3.2 Financing Structure and Investment Scale

The Piraeus project demonstrates diversified yet commercially-oriented financing characteristics. Unlike typical BRI projects in other regions relying heavily on policy-based lending, Piraeus relied primarily on COSCO Shipping's own funds and Chinese domestic commercial bank loans. 比雷埃夫斯项目展示了多元化但面向商业的融资特征。不同于其他地区典型BRI项目严重依赖政策性贷款，比雷埃夫斯主要依赖中远海运自有资金和的中国国内商业银行贷款。

4.3.3 Rule Interaction Practice with EU Regulations

As a BRI project located within EU territory, Piraeus was required to comply with EU legal and regulatory requirements, rendering it a paradigm case for observing BRI-EU rule interaction. COSCO Shipping's approach exemplifies "adaptive compliance"—proactive acceptance of EU constraints rather than confrontation. 作为位于欧盟领土内的BRI项目，比雷埃夫斯必须遵守欧盟法律法规要求，使其成为观察BRI-EU规则互动的范例案例。中远海运的做法体现了"适应性合规"——主动接受欧盟约束而非对抗。

1. Foreign Investment Screening

COSCO's acquisition underwent review by the EU Competition Commission. Approved in 2016 as not significantly harming market competition. 中远的收购接受了欧盟竞争委员会审查。2016年获批，认定不会严重损害市场竞争。

Smoking-gun Evidence

2. Environmental Standards

Pier expansion required EU Environmental Impact Assessment (EIA) Directive compliance. COSCO committed: "We will cherish every tree and blade of grass here as we cherish our own eyes." 码头扩建需符合欧盟环境影响评估(EIA)指令。中远承诺："我们会像珍惜自己的眼睛一样珍惜这里的每一棵树和每一株草。"

Hoop Evidence

3. Procurement & Transparency

PPA procurement complied with EU Procurement Directives. Negotiations featured "open discussion, repeated deliberation; sometimes a single clause negotiated back and forth for two or three months." PPA采购遵守欧盟采购指令。谈判特点是"公开讨论、反复审议；有时一个条款来回谈判两三个月。"

Hoop Evidence

4. Labor Standards

Committed to avoiding mass layoffs, absorbing redundant staff through new business development. Respected local union power—critical for social license. 承诺避免大规模裁员，通过新业务开发吸收冗余人员。尊重当地工会力量——这对社会许可至关重要。

Straw Evidence

Ongoing Compliance Challenge: Pier IV Suspension (Feb 2026)

Even after a decade of success, regulatory scrutiny persists. ESAL approved €611.8m investment plan but suspended €300m Pier IV application, citing "conditions not yet ripe." COSCO responded by stating it would "continue communication with authorities" rather than confrontation—demonstrating sustained adaptive compliance. 即使经过十年的成功，监管审查依然存在。ESAL批准了6.118亿欧元的投资计划，但暂停了3亿欧元的四号码头申请，理由是"条件尚未成熟"。中远回应称将"继续与当局沟通"而非对抗——展示了持续的适应性合规。

4.3.7 Latest Developments and Ongoing Compliance Challenges (2024–2025)

Critical Update: Pier IV Investment Suspension (February 2026)

In February 2026, the Ethniki Simvoulio Alimenos Limenon (ESAL—National Council for Ports) approved COSCO Shipping's €611.8 million investment plan but suspended the €300 million application for the fourth container terminal, citing "conditions not yet ripe." COSCO Shipping expressed hope that authorities would "review the container terminal expansion project as soon as possible." 2026年2月，国家港口规划与发展委员会(ESAL)批准了中远海运6.118亿欧元的投资计划，但暂停了3亿欧元第四集装箱码头的申请，理由是"条件尚未成熟"。中远海运表示希望当局"尽快审查集装箱码头扩建项目"。

Analytical Significance of Pier IV Suspension

1. Persistence of Scrutiny

Demonstrates that even successful BRI projects face persistent regulatory scrutiny in EU environments—not "one-time compliance, permanent validity."

2. Balancing Act

Reflects Greek authorities' balancing between national economic interests and EU competition policy concerns—Pier IV might further strengthen COSCO's dominant position.

3. Sustained Adaptation

COSCO's response ("continue communication" rather than confrontation) demonstrates that adaptive compliance has been internalized as organizational routine.

Operational Results (2024)

  • Container throughput: 6.5 million TEU (+3.5% from 2023), maintaining status as Mediterranean's largest and Europe's fourth-largest container port
  • Cumulative GDP contribution: Exceeded €800 million (IOBE updated assessment 2025)
  • Direct and indirect employment: Maintained at ~125,000 positions

Theoretical Implication / 理论启示

The February 2026 ESAL suspension validates the theoretical expectation that harmonious coordination requires sustained effort—even after a decade of successful operation, project expansion still faces regulatory review rather than "one-time compliance, permanent validity." COSCO Shipping's response strategy demonstrates that adaptive compliance has been internalized as organizational routine rather than mere tactical choice. 2026年2月ESAL的暂停验证了理论预期：和谐协调需要持续努力——即使经过十年的成功运营，项目扩建仍面临监管审查，而非"一次合规，永久有效"。中远海运的回应策略表明，适应性合规已被内化为组织常规，而不仅仅是战术选择。
