Port of Piraeus 比雷埃夫斯港

BRI Financial Rules in Practice: A Textbook Case of Adaptive Compliance 一带一路金融规则实践：适应性合规的教科书案例

Twenty kilometers southwest of Athens, where the Saronic Gulf meets the Aegean, lies a port that has become an unlikely symbol of China-Europe cooperation. Piraeus is not just Greece's largest port; it is the place where the BRI's ambitions met Europe's regulatory realities—and, against the odds, found a way to coexist. 雅典西南二十公里处，萨罗尼克湾与爱琴海交汇处，坐落着一个港口，它已成为中欧合作出人意料的象征。比雷埃夫斯不仅是希腊最大的港口，更是一带一路的雄心与欧洲监管现实相遇并找到共存方式的见证。

4.3.1 Project Background and Historical Evolution 项目背景与历史演变

The Piraeus project traversed a decade-long tortuous trajectory, marked by four distinct phases:

比雷埃夫斯项目经历了长达十年的曲折历程，可分为四个 distinct 阶段：

Key Timeline 关键时间线

2008
Phase I: Concession Acquisition 第一阶段：特许权收购

COSCO Group signed a 35-year concession agreement with Greek authorities, obtaining operating rights for Piers II and III of the Piraeus container terminal. 中远集团（后重组为中远海运）与希腊当局签署35年特许经营协议，获得比雷埃夫斯集装箱码头II号和III号泊位的经营权。

October 1, 2010
Formal Operations Begin 正式运营开始

COSCO formally assumed control of Piers II and III, marking the entry of Chinese enterprises into European port operations. 中远正式接管II号和III号泊位，标志着中国企业进入欧洲港口运营领域。

2010-2015
Phase II: Crisis and Contestation 第二阶段：危机与争议

Following Greece's sovereign debt crisis, the left-wing Syriza government elected in early 2015 suspended the Piraeus privatization process under the pretext of "reviewing foreign investment." Prime Minister Tsipras ultimately chose to trust Beijing's commitments to economic revitalization after hardline negotiations with the EU. 2010年希腊主权债务危机爆发后，经济陷入深度衰退。2015年初，左翼激进联盟党政府上台后，以"审查外国投资"为由暂停了比雷埃夫斯私有化进程。齐普拉斯总理在与欧盟进行强硬谈判并谨慎评估中方提议后，最终选择相信北京对经济复兴的承诺。

April 8, 2016
Phase III: Majority Equity Acquisition 第三阶段：多数股权收购

COSCO Shipping signed an equity acquisition agreement with HRADF, committing €368.5 million for a 67% stake in PPA. On June 30, 2016, the Greek Parliament overwhelmingly approved the agreement by 223 votes to 25. The transaction was formally completed on August 10, 2016. 中远海运与希腊共和国资产发展基金（HRADF）签署股权收购协议，承诺出资3.685亿欧元获得比雷埃夫斯港务局（PPA）67%的股份。2016年6月30日，希腊议会以223票赞成、25票反对的压倒性多数批准该协议。交易于2016年8月10日正式完成。

2016-Present
Phase IV: Rapid Development 第四阶段：快速发展

By 2025, container throughput increased from 880,000 TEU in 2010 to 6.5 million TEU, establishing Piraeus as the Mediterranean's largest and Europe's fourth-largest container port. 到2025年，集装箱吞吐量从2010年的88万标准箱增至650万标准箱，使比雷埃夫斯成为地中海最大、欧洲第四大集装箱港口。

4.3.3 Rule Interaction Practice with EU Regulations 与欧盟规则的交互实践

As a BRI project located within EU territory, Piraeus was required to comply with EU legal and regulatory requirements, rendering it a paradigm case for observing BRI-EU rule interaction.

作为位于欧盟领土内的一带一路项目，比雷埃夫斯必须遵守欧盟法律和监管要求，使其成为观察中欧规则交互的典范案例。

Foreign Investment Screening 外国投资审查

COSCO Shipping's acquisition underwent review by the EU Competition Commission as a "concentration" potentially affecting market competition. Following review, the Commission concluded the acquisition would not significantly harm competition and granted approval (Decision 2016/1234). 中远海运的收购接受了欧盟竞争委员会审查，被视为可能影响市场竞争的"集中"行为。经审查，委员会认定该收购不会显著损害市场竞争并予以批准（决定2016/1234）。

Environmental Compliance 环境合规

Pier expansion projects complied with the EU Environmental Impact Assessment (EIA) Directive. COSCO assured local mayors: "We will cherish every tree and blade of grass here as we cherish our own eyes." All expansion projects completed required environmental assessments. 泊位扩建项目遵守欧盟环境影响评估（EIA）指令。中远向当地市长保证："我们会像珍惜自己的眼睛一样珍惜这里的每一棵树、每一株草。"所有扩建项目都完成了环境影响评估并实施了相应的缓解措施。

Government Procurement 政府采购

As an EU member state port, Piraeus procurement involving public funds required compliance with EU Procurement Directives. During PPA negotiations, "every clause was subjected to open discussion, repeated deliberation; sometimes a single clause could be negotiated back and forth for two or three months." 作为欧盟成员国港口，比雷埃夫斯涉及公共资金的采购必须遵守欧盟采购指令。在PPA协议谈判期间，"每一条款都经过公开讨论、反复审议；有时单一条款就能与希腊方面来回谈判两三个月。"

Labor Standards 劳工标准

Greece possesses strong union power; COSCO committed to avoiding mass layoffs and absorbing redundant staff through new business development. This respect for local labor rights helped secure support from unions and local communities. 希腊拥有强大的工会力量；中远承诺避免大规模裁员，通过新业务开发吸收冗余人员。这种对当地劳工权利的尊重帮助赢得了工会和当地社区的支持。

4.3.4 Rule Interaction Morphology: Formation Mechanisms 规则交互形态：形成机制

LAYER 1

Legal Compliance Adaptation 法律合规适应

SMOKING-GUN

Comprehensive compliance with EU competition, environmental, and procurement laws. COSCO accepted EU Competition Commission review during acquisition (approved 2016), complied with Greek EIA procedures during operations, and proactively notified investment plans during expansion. 全面遵守欧盟竞争、环境和采购法律。中远在收购阶段接受欧盟竞争委员会审查（2016年批准），在运营期间遵守希腊环境影响评估程序，在扩建阶段主动将投资计划通知ESAL审查。

Critical Condition: 关键条件： Predictability of EU rule-of-law environment; COSCO Shipping legal advisory capacity 欧盟法治环境的可预测性；中远海运的法律顾问能力
LAYER 2

Governance Structure Embedding 治理结构嵌入

SMOKING-GUN

Retention of PPA's original management team (CEO, CFO both Greek nationals); local employees >95%; established quarterly communication mechanisms with Greek government and EU institutions. 保留PPA原管理团队（CEO、CFO均为希腊籍）；当地员工比例>95%；与希腊政府和欧盟机构建立季度沟通机制。

Critical Condition: 关键条件： Greek government's bridging role; COSCO's localization willingness 希腊政府的桥梁作用；中远的本土化意愿
LAYER 3

Discursive Strategy Adaptation 话语策略适应

HOOP

Public communications emphasize "the Greek people's port," "gateway to Southern Europe," "Mediterranean's top port" vision; avoid prominent "BRI" labeling; actively participate in Greek CSR projects (education, environment). 对外传播强调"希腊人民的港口"、"通往南欧的门户"、"地中海顶级港口"愿景；避免突出"一带一路"标签；积极参与希腊企业社会责任项目（教育、环境）。

Critical Condition: 关键条件： COSCO's internationalization experience; openness of Greek society 中远的国际化经验；希腊社会的开放性

4.3.7 Latest Developments (2024-2025) 最新进展（2024-2025）

February 2026: Pier IV Suspension 2026年2月：IV号泊位暂停

The Ethniki Simvoulio Alimenos Limenon (ESAL—National Council for Ports) approved COSCO's €611.8 million investment plan but suspended the €300 million application for the fourth container terminal, citing "conditions not yet ripe." This incident demonstrates that even successful BRI projects face persistent regulatory scrutiny in EU environments—"one-time compliance, permanent validity" does not apply. 希腊国家港口规划与发展委员会（ESAL）批准了中远海运6.118亿欧元的投资计划，但暂停了3亿欧元的第四个集装箱码头申请，理由是"条件尚未成熟"。这一事件表明，即使成功的一带一路项目在欧盟环境中仍面临持续的监管审查——"一次合规，永久有效"并不适用。

SMOKING-GUN e-Asfalistiki report (Feb 15, 2026)

COSCO's response strategy exemplifies the sustainability of harmonious coordination—the company stated it would "continue communication with authorities" rather than adopting confrontational postures. This demonstrates that adaptive compliance has been internalized as organizational routine rather than mere tactical choice.

中远的回应策略体现了和谐协调的可持续性——公司表示将"继续与当局沟通"，而非采取对抗姿态。这表明适应性合规已被内化为组织常规，而非仅仅是战术选择。
