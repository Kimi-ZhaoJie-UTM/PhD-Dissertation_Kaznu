Chapter 01

引言：制度复杂性下的中欧基建倡议对接

Introduction: Institutional Complexity in China-EU Infrastructure Initiative Interaction

TL;DR: 本研究探讨当BRI（一带一路）与Global Gateway在全球基建领域相遇时，制度复杂性如何塑造规则互动形态（和谐协调/冲突对抗/战略套利），并基于8维度框架与双案例分析提出战略决策算法。

1.1 研究背景与问题提出 | Research Background

2021年12月，欧盟委员会正式启动\"全球门户\"（Global Gateway）战略，计划投入€3000亿用于全球基础设施投资。这一倡议被广泛视为对\"一带一路\"（BRI）的战略回应——后者自2013年提出以来，已在全球形成庞大的制度网络。

核心研究问题：

在制度复杂性环境下，BRI与Global Gateway的规则互动呈现何种形态？何种条件导致\"和谐协调\"、\"冲突对抗\"或\"战略套利\"三种差异化结果？

In December 2021, the European Commission officially launched the Global Gateway strategy, committing €300 billion for global infrastructure investment. This initiative is widely viewed as a strategic response to the Belt and Road Initiative (BRI), which has established a vast institutional network since 2013.

Core Research Question:

Under conditions of institutional complexity, what forms of rule interaction emerge between BRI and Global Gateway? Under what conditions do "harmonious coordination," "conflictual confrontation," or "strategic arbitrage" result?
