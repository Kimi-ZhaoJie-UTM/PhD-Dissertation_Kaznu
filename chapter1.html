Chapter 1 / 第一章

Introduction and Theoretical Foundations 引言与理论基础 Introduction and Theoretical Foundations
引言与理论基础

This chapter establishes the theoretical scaffolding upon which subsequent empirical analysis rests. Rather than treating the Belt and Road Initiative (BRI) as a monolithic geopolitical project or merely a financing mechanism, I conceptualize it as an emergent governance regime operating within an increasingly complex institutional environment.

本章为后续实证分析奠定理论基础。不同于将"一带一路"倡议(BRI)视为单一的地缘政治项目或单纯的融资机制，我将其概念化为一个在日益复杂的制度环境中运作的新兴治理制度。

1.1 Assessment of the Strategic Objectives of the Belt and Road Initiative 1.1 "一带一路"倡议战略目标评估 1.1 Assessment of Strategic Objectives / 战略目标评估

Before proceeding, a threshold question must be addressed: are the BRI and Global Gateway genuinely comparable? Skeptics might object that we are comparing apples and oranges—one is a decade-old, project-driven initiative involving over 150 countries; the other is a fledgling, strategy-driven framework still finding its operational feet. The objection is fair but misses the point.

What makes the two regimes comparable is not their scale or maturity, but their functional equivalence: both are transnational infrastructure initiatives that offer financing to third countries, both operate in the same geographic spaces (Eurasia, Africa), and both seek to shape the rules of the game in development finance.

在继续之前，必须解决一个根本问题：BRI与全球门户(Global Gateway)是否真的具有可比性？怀疑者可能会反对，认为我们在比较苹果和橘子——一个是拥有150多个国家参与的、以项目为导向的十年老倡议；另一个是仍在寻找操作立足点的、以战略为导向的新生框架。这种反对是公平的，但错过了要点。

使这两个制度可比的并非其规模或成熟度，而是其功能等价性：两者都是向第三国提供融资的跨国基础设施倡议，都在相同的地理空间（欧亚、非洲）运作，都寻求在发展融资领域塑造游戏规则。

The "Belt and Road" initiative was proposed by China in 2013. It is a cross-border initiative aimed at promoting connectivity and common development among the countries along the routes. Its core concept is the "five connectivity"—policy coordination, infrastructure connection, unimpeded trade, financial communication, and people-to-people connection.

"一带一路"倡议由中国于2013年提出。这是一个旨在促进沿线国家互联互通与共同发展的跨境倡议。其核心概念是"五通"——政策沟通、设施联通、贸易畅通、资金融通和民心相通。

Three Developmental Phases / 三个发展阶段 三个发展阶段

Phase I (2013–2017) / 第一阶段

Macro-Framework Construction

宏观框架构建

Establishing foundational principles and cooperative architecture. The 2015 publication of Vision and Actions systematically articulated the BRI's top-level design.

建立基础原则与合作架构。2015年《愿景与行动》的发布系统阐述了BRI的顶层设计。

Phase II (2018–2020) / 第二阶段

Project Implementation & Risk Recognition

项目实施与风险认知

Flagship projects progressed from planning to execution. International scrutiny intensified regarding debt sustainability, transparency, and environmental compliance.

旗舰项目从规划进入执行。国际社会对债务可持续性、透明度和环境合规性的审查加剧。

Phase III (2021–Present) / 第三阶段

High-Quality Development

高质量发展

Strategic pivot toward "high-quality development". By 2025, cooperation agreements with ~150 countries, cumulative investment reaching $1.308 trillion.

战略转向"高质量发展"。截至2025年，与约150个国家签署合作协议，累计投资达1.308万亿美元。

1.2 Financial Mechanisms and Investment Instruments of the BRI 1.2 "一带一路"的金融机制与投资工具 1.2 Financial Mechanisms / 金融机制与投资工具

Financial cooperation constitutes the critical infrastructure supporting the BRI's operational implementation. Over the past decade, the initiative has evolved a diversified toolkit of financing instruments, encompassing policy-based lending, concessional loans, commercial credit, equity investment funds, bond issuance, and local-currency settlement mechanisms.

金融合作构成了支撑BRI运营实施的关键基础设施。在过去十年中，该倡议已发展出多样化的工具包，包括政策性贷款、优惠贷款、商业信贷、股权投资基金、债券发行和本币结算机制。

1.2.1 Policy-Based Lending and Concessional Lending 1.2.1 政策性贷款与优惠贷款 1.2.1 Policy-Based Lending / 政策性贷款与优惠贷款

Policy-based lending has historically constituted the primary funding source for early-phase BRI infrastructure projects. The China Development Bank (CDB) and the Export-Import Bank of China (China Exim Bank), as the two principal policy financial institutions, provide long-term, large-scale credit support.

According to AidData statistics, between 2000 and 2021, Chinese development finance institutions globally provided 13,427 development financing projects totaling approximately $843 billion.

政策性贷款历来构成早期BRI基础设施项目的主要资金来源。中国国家开发银行(CDB)和中国进出口银行作为两大政策性金融机构，提供长期、大规模的信贷支持。

根据AidData统计，2000年至2021年间，中国开发性金融机构在全球范围内提供了13,427个发展融资项目，总额约8430亿美元。

Distinguishing Characteristics / distinguishing特征 distinguishing特征

  • Extended maturities: 15-20 years, some to 30 years
  • Concessional pricing: Below-market benchmarks
  • Project-linked conditionality: Earmarked for Chinese contractors
  • 延长期限：15-20年，部分可达30年
  • 优惠定价：低于市场基准
  • 项目关联条件性：指定用于中国承包商

1.2.2 Equity Investment and Funds 1.2.2 股权投资与基金 1.2.2 Equity Investment / 股权投资与基金

The establishment of the Silk Road Fund in 2014 marked China's inaugural equity investment vehicle specifically dedicated to BRI objectives. With initial capital of $40 billion (subsequently increased to $45 billion), the Fund operates through equity participation, joint ventures, and project-specific capitalization.

丝路基金于2014年成立，标志着中国首个专门致力于BRI目标的股权投资工具的诞生。初始资本为400亿美元（随后增至450亿美元），该基金通过股权参与、合资企业和项目特定资本化进行运作。

Key Operational Characteristics

主要运作特征

  • Market-driven governance: Commercial viability focus
  • Portfolio diversification: Infrastructure, energy, industrial capacity
  • Leverage effects: Catalytic capital deployment
  • 市场化治理：注重商业可行性
  • 投资组合多元化：基础设施、能源、产能合作
  • 杠杆效应：催化性资本配置

By year-end 2024: 200+ projects, $20B+ committed 截至2024年底：200多个项目，承诺投资超200亿美元

1.2.3 Multilateral Financial Institutions 1.2.3 多边金融机构 1.2.3 Multilateral Institutions / 多边金融机构

The establishment of the Asian Infrastructure Investment Bank (AIIB) in 2015 provided a crucial complement to the BRI's financial architecture. Operating as a multilateral development bank (MDB), the AIIB introduces internationally recognized environmental, social, and procurement standards.

亚洲基础设施投资银行(AIIB)于2015年成立，为BRI金融架构提供了重要补充。作为多边开发银行(MDB)运作，AIIB引入了国际认可的环境、社会和采购标准。

By year-end 2025: 110 countries, 300+ projects approved, $50B+ financing

截至2025年底：110个成员国，批准300多个项目，融资超500亿美元

1.2.4 Bond Financing and Financial Innovation 1.2.4 债券融资与金融创新 1.2.4 Bond Financing / 债券融资与金融创新

In recent years, the BRI financing toolkit has witnessed continuous innovation, with Panda bonds emerging as a significant modality. Panda bonds—renminbi-denominated bonds issued by foreign entities within China's domestic capital markets—enable project financing with currency-asset matching, thereby reducing foreign exchange risk exposure.

近年来，BRI融资工具包不断创新，熊猫债券已成为一种重要模式。熊猫债券——在中国国内资本市场发行的以人民币计价的债券——使项目融资能够实现货币-资产匹配，从而降低外汇风险敞口。

Research by Schclarek et al. demonstrates that financing BRI projects through Panda bond issuance can effectively mitigate currency mismatches, as future project revenues are frequently generated in renminbi or local currencies correlated with the Chinese currency.

Schclarek等人的研究表明，通过熊猫债券发行为BRI项目融资可以有效缓解货币错配，因为未来的项目收益经常以人民币或与人民币相关的本币产生。

1.3 Theoretical Framework: Institutional Complexity and Regulatory Coopetition 1.3 理论框架：制度复杂性与监管竞合 1.3 Theoretical Framework / 理论框架：制度复杂性与监管竞合

1.3.1 Institutional Complexity Theory and Its Evolution 1.3.1 制度复杂性理论及其演进 1.3.1 Institutional Complexity / 制度复杂性理论

How do we make sense of the tangled relationship between the BRI and the Global Gateway? Institutional complexity theory offers a way in. Born from international relations debates about "regime overlap," this framework stops treating institutions as isolated islands and starts asking how actors navigate the spaces between them.

我们如何理解BRI与全球门户之间错综复杂的关系？制度复杂性理论提供了一种切入点。源于国际关系关于"制度重叠"的辩论，这一框架不再将制度视为孤立的岛屿，而是开始询问行为者如何在其间的空间中航行。

Three Ideal Morphologies (Orsini et al.) 三种理想形态 (Orsini等)

Institutional Overlapping Multiple institutions cover the same policy domain with different rule designs. BRI and Global Gateway coexistence exemplifies this.
制度重叠 多个机构覆盖相同政策领域但规则设计不同。BRI与全球门户的共存即为例证。
Institutional Nesting Local institutions embed within global architectures. BRI projects in EU markets must embed within EU legal systems.
制度嵌套 地方机构嵌入全球架构。欧盟市场中的BRI项目必须嵌入欧盟法律体系。
Competition/Cooperation Different institutions provide alternative rule choices, enabling "forum shopping" or synergies.
竞争/合作 不同机构提供替代性规则选择，使"论坛购物"或协同效应成为可能。

1.3.2 The "Coopetition" Concept 1.3.2 "竞合"概念 1.3.2 The "Coopetition" Concept / "竞合"概念

Within the institutional complexity framework, the relationship between BRI and Global Gateway can be characterized as "coopetition"—a concept originally developed by Brandenburger and Nalebuff to describe interactive patterns wherein actors simultaneously compete and cooperate.

在制度复杂性框架内，BRI与全球门户之间的关系可以定性为"竞合"(coopetition)——这一概念最初由Brandenburger和Nalebuff提出，用以描述行为者同时竞争与合作的互动模式。

Competitive Dimensions
竞争维度
  • • Rule competition (flexibility vs. high standards)
  • • 规则竞争（灵活性vs高标准）
  • • Financing competition (project resources)
  • • 融资竞争（项目资源）
  • • Geopolitical competition (influence expansion)
  • • 地缘政治竞争（影响力扩张）
Cooperative Dimensions
合作维度
  • • Project-level cooperation in third markets
  • • 第三市场的项目级合作
  • • Rule coordination in green finance
  • • 绿色金融中的规则协调
  • • Shared challenge response (climate change)
  • • 共同挑战应对（气候变化）

1.3.4 Typology of "Rule Interaction" [Core Innovation] 1.3.4 "规则互动"类型学 [核心创新] 1.3.4 Typology of Rule Interaction / 规则互动类型学

Based on institutional complexity theory, this study constructs three ideal types of "rule interaction" as theoretical tools for analyzing the BRI–Global Gateway relationship.

基于制度复杂性理论，本研究构建了三种理想类型的"规则互动"作为分析BRI-全球门户关系的理论工具。

Type I: Harmonious Coordination 类型一：协调性协调 (适应性协调)

Scenarios wherein BRI projects proactively adapt to EU regulatory requirements. Core characteristic: "Adaptive compliance"—accepting EU constraints without sacrificing efficiency.

BRI项目主动适应欧盟监管要求的场景。核心特征："适应性合规"——接受欧盟约束而不牺牲效率。

Case: Port of Piraeus 案例：比雷埃夫斯港

Type II: Conflictual Confrontation 类型二：冲突性对抗

Projects encounter rule mutual exclusivity. Core characteristic: "Regulatory friction"—substantive conflicts between rule systems.

项目遭遇规则互斥的场景。核心特征："监管摩擦"——规则体系间的实质性冲突。

Case: Budapest–Belgrade Railway 案例：布达佩斯-贝尔格莱德铁路

Type III: Strategic Arbitrage 类型三：战略性套利

Countries exploit differences to select advantageous rules. Core characteristic: "Institutional shopping"—strategic selection among environments.

国家利用差异选择有利规则的场景。核心特征："制度购物"——在环境中的策略性选择。

Case: Kazakhstan (Nurly Zhol) 案例：哈萨克斯坦（光明之路）

1.4.1 Impacts on Asian Countries 1.4.1 对亚洲国家的影响 1.4.1 Asian Impacts / 对亚洲国家的影响

Asia represents the most concentrated region for BRI investment. According to GMF statistics, Chinese infrastructure investment in Asia accounted for over 60 percent of total overseas investment (2000-2025).

亚洲是BRI投资最集中的地区。根据GMF统计，2000-2025年间，中国在亚洲的基础设施投资占海外投资总额的60%以上。

Central Asia / 中亚

Kazakhstan: 50+ projects, $20B+ investment under China-Kazakhstan Industrial Capacity Cooperation.

哈萨克斯坦：产能合作框架下50多个项目，投资超200亿美元。

Southeast Asia / 东南亚

China-Laos Railway: 40% freight cost reduction, 2-3 days to 3-4 hours travel time.

中老铁路：货运成本降低40%，行程时间从2-3天缩短至3-4小时。

South Asia / 南亚

CPEC: Over $25 billion accumulated investment in Pakistan.

中巴经济走廊(CPEC)：在巴基斯坦累计投资超250亿美元。

1.4.2 Impacts on European Countries 1.4.2 对欧洲国家的影响 1.4.2 European Impacts / 对欧洲国家的影响

European participation exhibits East-West differentiation. The Port of Piraeus in Greece represents the most successful BRI case—container throughput increased from 880,000 TEU in 2010 to 6.5 million TEU in 2024.

欧洲参与呈现东西分化。希腊比雷埃夫斯港代表欧洲最成功的BRI案例——集装箱吞吐量从2010年的88万TEU增长到2024年的650万TEU。

1.4.3 Heterogeneity: Role of Institutional Factors 1.4.3 异质性：制度因素的调节作用 1.4.3 Institutional Factors / 异质性与制度因素

Hoong et al. employ PMG-ARDL analysis across nine Asian countries, finding that BRI engagement produces negative long-term effects on government debt-to-GDP ratios (reducing debt levels), though short-term effects vary according to national governance capacity.

Hoong等人采用PMG-ARDL方法对九个亚洲国家进行分析，发现BRI参与对政府债务/GDP比率产生负的长期影响（降低债务水平），尽管短期效应因国家治理能力而异。

1.5 Research Design 1.5 研究设计 1.5 Research Design / 研究设计

1.5.1 Methodological Positioning 1.5.1 方法论定位 1.5.1 Methodology / 方法论定位

Every research design is a product of trade-offs. I opt for a mixed-methods approach combining comparative institutional analysis with case study methodology—not because it is the most "standard" choice, but because it best fits the research question: how do rule differences translate into financial opportunities and risks?

每一种研究设计都是权衡的产物。我选择混合方法，将比较制度分析与案例研究相结合——并非因为它是最"标准"的选择，而是因为它最适合研究问题：规则差异如何转化为金融机遇与风险？

Three Reasons Against Large-N Statistics 反对大样本统计的三个理由

  1. Time window too short (BRI 2013, Global Gateway 2021)
  2. Mechanisms matter more than correlations
  3. Institutional details cannot be compressed into variables
  1. 时间窗口太短（BRI始于2013，全球门户始于2021）
  2. 机制比相关性更重要
  3. 制度细节不能压缩为变量

本研究选择比雷埃夫斯港和布达佩斯-贝尔格莱德铁路，基于"最相似"背景与"最不同"结果的逻辑。

1.5.4 Research Limitations 1.5.4 研究局限 1.5.4 Limitations / 研究局限

Limited case quantity: Only two cases; findings may not apply universally. Response: Chapter 5 discusses Asian cases for cross-regional testing.
案例数量有限：仅两个案例；发现可能不具普遍性。应对：第五章讨论亚洲案例进行跨区域检验。
Dependence on public sources: No field interviews. Response: Utilize semi-public data and multi-source triangulation.
依赖公开资料：无实地访谈。应对：利用半公开资料和多源三角验证。
Temporal constraints: Global Gateway implementation period short (2021–present). Response: Define boundaries through end-2025.
时间限制：全球门户实施期短（2021至今）。应对：界定时间边界至2025年底。

Summary: This study employs mixed methods combining comparative institutional analysis with case studies. Through "most similar" and "most different" case selection logic, the research controls background variables to focus on the impact of rule interaction morphology on project performance.

小结：本研究采用混合方法，结合比较制度分析与案例研究。通过"最相似"和"最不同"的案例选择逻辑，控制背景变量，聚焦于规则互动形态对项目绩效的影响。
